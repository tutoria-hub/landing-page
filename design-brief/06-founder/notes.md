## Photo Specifications Reminder

**Required**:
- Format: JPG or PNG
- Dimensions: Minimum 320x320px (recommend 480x480px)
- Crop: Square (1:1 aspect ratio)
- Composition: Face centered, clean background
- Lighting: Well-lit, natural light preferred
- Style: Professional or friendly casual

**To Avoid**:
- Group photos cropped to individual
- Low-resolution selfies
- Busy backgrounds
- Dark/underexposed photos
