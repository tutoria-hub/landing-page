## Purpose

Humanize the product and build trust through personal connection.

Shows real people behind Tutoria, establishes credibility through lived experience.

Answers: "Who built this and why should I trust them?"

---

## Key Components

1. **Section Header**: "The great minds behind Tutoria" (48px/32px Nunito Bold)
2. **Founder Grid**: 2-column layout (circular photos)
   - Photo: 160px diameter circles
   - Green border (#30A46C, 2px)
   - Name: 24px Nunito Bold
   - Title: 16px Nunito Regular
3. **Founder Story**: Personal dyslexia story one-liner (18px italic)

---

## Assets Required

- Founder photos (480x480px minimum, square crop)
- Founder names and titles
- Personal story one-liner (50-100 characters)
